About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website! As a creative and innovative thinker, I am passionate about bringing ideas to life through strategic thinking, innovation management, and design thinking. With expertise in product and graphic design, digital illustration, content creation, and video editing, I deliver high-quality projects that meet and exceed expectations.

Professional Background

In my role as a Vocational Education Officer at Ben-janet Educational Services, I have organized successful workshops, managed arts and vocational education programs, and co-organized the Arts Scholar program, which brought together 30 learners from 10 public primary schools in Abeokuta South L.G. Additionally, designed the Cre8crib.co project, improving access to vocational education in my community.

Creative Ventures

Outside of my professional experience, I am passionate about writing and expressing my creativity through the written word. This interest in writing has helped me excel as a scriptwriter and contribute significantly to the digital marketing efforts of Apex of Arts and Theater, where I was a key member of the production team.

Educational Background

My educational journey includes: - Master of Education in Fine and Applied Arts from Tai Solarin University of Education - Bachelor of Arts in Creative Arts - Several certifications from leading institutions such as Google, the University of Sydney, and Terra Academy for the Arts

Recognition

In 2016, I was honored with the Devine Connect Award for Best Arts Teacher in Ogun state, a testament to my dedication and hard work in the field of education.

Commitment to Growth

I am always seeking new opportunities to learn and grow, and I am excited to use my skills and experience to help others achieve their goals. Through my work in design, education, and writing, I am committed to making a positive impact in my community.
